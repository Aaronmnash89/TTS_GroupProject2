How to Dust the House


Get your dust rag and dusting spray, then carefully go over the furniture that has collected dust, making sure to not miss any spot. Be cognizant of any breakables. Some find it easier to remove delicate items before starting.


How to Sweep the Floors


Once you've completed your dusting, grab that broom and get crackin'. Sweep under and around furniture, using the dust pan as needed to help keep yourself organized in the middle of the mess.
